# Video Gen Concepts & Runway/Pika Prompts

**Target Use:** Background looping video (B-Roll) for the stage, and cutaways for social media promotion.

## Shot 1: The Matrix Xerox
**Model:** Runway Gen-2 / Pika Labs
**Prompt:** Extreme close up macro shot of a Xerox machine scanning a piece of paper, the harsh white light sliding across the glass, but the paper being scanned is transforming into digital matrix code. Gritty, 16mm film, high contrast, cyberpunk aesthetic.
**Motion:** Slow, steady lateral panning.

## Shot 2: The Sound System Data Center
**Model:** Runway Gen-2 / Pika Labs
**Prompt:** A massive wall of traditional Jamaican sound system speaker boxes vibrating with heavy bass, but the speakers are seamlessly integrated into glowing, rack-mounted corporate AI servers. Cold blue lights clashing with warm Jamaican club lighting. Heavy film grain, kinetic energy.
**Motion:** Subwoofer vibration, slow zoom out.

## Shot 3: The 1,800 Photos
**Model:** Runway Gen-2 / Pika Labs
**Prompt:** A vortex of thousands of polaroid photos spinning rapidly in a tornado, being sucked into a massive glowing digital eye representing artificial intelligence. Photorealistic, dramatic lighting, high contrast.
**Motion:** Fast chaotic spinning converging on the center.
