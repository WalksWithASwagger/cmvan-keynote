# ElevenLabs / HeyGen Dress Rehearsal Script

*This is a clean, spoken version adapted for ElevenLabs voice synthesis reading.*

Every cultural movement that actually mattered started the exact same way. Someone picks up a tool built by a massive corporation—and they use it wrong. 

The Dadaists did it. The Jamaican Selectors did it. The kids printing zines on Xerox machines in basements did it. They took the infrastructure of the spectacle, and they turned it against itself. 

AI is just our generation's Xerox machine. It's built by the biggest corporations on earth. It's trained on stolen work—including about 1,800 of my own photos. But here's the messy part that nobody wants to admit: using these tools, I am also more creative, and more powerful, than I have ever been in my entire life. Both of those statements are true. And that is the punk condition.

It’s complex. It’s gritty. And it’s exactly what I’m talking about at Creative Mornings Vancouver this May. I’m going to give you a punk rock framework to engage with Artificial Intelligence without selling your soul. 

Release Day is May 29th. Generation is cheap. Taste is not.

You coming?
