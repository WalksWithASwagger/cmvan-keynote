# Multimedia Generation Specs
**Creative Mornings Vancouver — May 1, 2026 (talk) / May 29, 2026 (Release Day)**

This document tracks the prompts and specifications needed for generating the background visual assets, slide deck images, and the promotional HeyGen / ElevenLabs dress-rehearsal video.

## 1. Midjourney / DALL-E Image Prompts
*Target Aesthetic:* High-contrast black and white, vintage 1970s Xerox zine texture mixed with cyberpunk/cybernetic elements, halftones, misaligned CMYK, glitch art.

**Prompt 1 (The Xerox Heritage):**
> A gritty black and white photo of an old oversized Xerox photocopy machine in a dingy 1970s London basement, but bursting out of the output tray is glowing, futuristic cyberpunk data streams. Mixed media collage, halftone print texture, high contrast, DIY punk zine aesthetic, rough edges, highly detailed --ar 16:9

**Prompt 2 (The Selector):**
> A vintage 1970s Jamaican sound system DJ clash, a selector standing in front of a massive wall of handmade speakers holding a glowing futuristic tablet instead of a vinyl record. High contrast black and white photography, heavy film grain, halftone texture, collage art, chaotic energy --ar 16:9

**Prompt 3 (The Data Center):**
> A massive, cold, endless corporate server farm fading into a pile of stolen art canvases and broken film cameras. Gritty, high contrast, surrealist corporate overreach, 1980s punk rock flyer aesthetic, photocopied texture --ar 16:9

## 2. Runway / Pika Video Prompts (B-Roll)
*Target Use:* Looping background video for walk-in / walk-out.

**Prompt 1:**
> Extreme close up macro shot of a Xerox machine scanning a piece of paper, the harsh white light sliding across the glass, but the paper being scanned is transforming into digital matrix code. Gritty, 16mm film, high contrast.

## 3. HeyGen / ElevenLabs Video Rehearsal Spec
*Goal:* Create a promotional hook or a dress-rehearsal AI avatar video to share on social media leading up to the **May 1** talk and through **Release Day (May 29)**.

**Voice Setup (ElevenLabs):**
- Use the cloned model of Kris's voice.
- Settings: High stability (70%), low clarity/similarity to introduce a slight digital grit, maybe over-drive the compression to sound like an old PA system.

**Avatar Setup (HeyGen):**
- Use the 'Casual Kris' custom avatar with a dark studio background (or a green screen, so you can key out the background and drop the avatar over the Midjourney Xerox collage).
