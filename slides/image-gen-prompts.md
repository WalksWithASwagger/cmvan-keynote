## 1. Midjourney / DALL-E — hero trio (walk-in / title energy)

*Target aesthetic:* High-contrast black and white, 1970s–90s Xerox zine, halftone, **blood red** accents, occasional **neon leak** only where noted — not generic “AI slop purple.”

**Prompt A — Cassette + future leak (Matlock energy):**

> Battered 90s cassette labeled "27 YEARS" in worn marker, extreme close-up on dirty fingers, background dissolving into glitching neon waveform and tiny floating "play" triangles like rogue UI. Black white blood red only except **thin** cyan-magenta chromatic aberration on glitch edges only. Heavy photocopy grain, DIY punk flyer, --ar 16:9

**Prompt B — Xerox vs the stack (your conflict):**

> Oversized 1970s Xerox machine in a basement, output tray vomiting a **torrent of printed photos** that mid-air turn into **binary rain**. One red handprint on the copier glass. High contrast, collage, halftone, caption zone left blank for post. --ar 16:9

**Prompt C — Selector + now (taste era):**

> Sound system wall of speakers, silhouette crowd, selector raising a **vinyl** in one hand and a **smartphone** in the other — anachronism on purpose. B/W with **one** red record label circle. Margin scrawls "CHEAP" "NOT CHEAP" crossed out and rewritten "TASTE". Film grain, zine paste-up, --ar 16:9

## 3. HeyGen / ElevenLabs — dress rehearsal

*Goal:* Promo / social leading up to **May 1** and through **Release Day (May 29)**.

**Voice (ElevenLabs):** cloned Kris voice; high stability (~70%); slight PA distortion optional.

**Avatar (HeyGen):** Casual Kris; dark or greenscreen; composite over **Prompt B** collage.

**Script:**

"Every movement that mattered started with someone picking up a corporate machine and using it wrong. Dada cut-ups. Sniffin' Glue on a stolen copier. Selectors choosing what the room needed when the sound got cheap.

AI is our Xerox — built by giants, trained on us without asking — including about eighteen hundred of my photos. And I'm still having the most creative year of my life. That's the punk condition.

I'm Kris Krüg. **May first**, Creative Mornings Vancouver — **Punk Rock AI**. Then the world keeps going: **Release Day, May twenty-ninth** — ship something honest. Generation is cheap. Taste is not. You coming?"
