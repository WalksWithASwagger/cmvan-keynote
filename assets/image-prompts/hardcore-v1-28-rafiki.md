# PUNK ROCK AI — Hardcore Slide Pack v1
## 28 Slides · Black Dominant · Blood Red + White Accents · Hardcore Punk
## Rafiki batch format — gemini-3-pro-image-preview

*Aesthetic: BLACK dominant backgrounds — tar black, void black, burnt charcoal. BLOOD RED as primary aggressive accent. WHITE as stark, high-contrast secondary. No pastels, no earth tones, no grays. Silkscreen printing effect with blown registration. Spray-can stencils. Hardcore flyer energy — Black Flag, Discharge, Crass, Dead Kennedys. Soviet expressionist propaganda meets DIY basement show. Distressed, burned, confrontational. Nothing pretty. Everything urgent.*
