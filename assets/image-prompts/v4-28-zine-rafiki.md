# PUNK ROCK AI — Zine Slide Pack v4

*Zine style: torn newspaper, xerox grain, contact sheets, film leader strips, halftone dots, hand-stamped text, cut-and-paste collage. B&W throughout. Blood red as the only accent color. No clean hero shots. No chrome, no data centers. Feels like something made at 2am in a copy shop that's about to close.*

**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Prompt:**
> A battered camera body sits on a nightstand or threshold — xeroxed at high contrast, slightly overexposed. Outside a window: streets, light, the world. The camera faces outward. Around it: fragments of hospital imagery (wires, monitors as silhouettes), dark and still. But the camera points toward the door. A hand-stamped label runs across the bottom: SURVIVAL DEVICE. Margin scrawl in small, urgent hand: "IT GOT ME OUT OF THE HOUSE." Film leader strip at the top. One blood red element: a single arrow pointing toward the door, toward the light, toward the streets. **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Prompt:**
> Two-panel composition, side by side on one xeroxed sheet. Left panel: a vast contact sheet, dense rows of thumbnail images spreading outward — the great release. Margin scrawl at the bottom: "FOR TWENTY YEARS THAT LOGIC WAS SOUND." Film leader between the two panels like a dividing strip. Blood red only on OBSCURITY. Grain throughout.
