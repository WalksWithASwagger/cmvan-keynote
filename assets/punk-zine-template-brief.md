# Release Day Activation: The Punk Zine Brief

**Purpose:** To anchor the "Release Day" challenge on May 29th, we will collect the creative outputs of the Creative Mornings Vancouver audience and compile them into a digital (and potentially physical) Punk Zine.

## Concept: "Digital Composting Meets Xerox Culture"
Instead of asking attendees to just "use AI," we are challenging them to "Release Something." The zine acts as the collective deadline and gallery space. It forces the audience to stop experimenting infinitely and start deciding.

### The Brief for the Audience
1. **The Task:** Use the month of May to externalize your creative taste into the machine. Generate one specific asset (a poem, a photo, an essay, a design). 
2. **The Criteria:** It doesn't have to be perfect. It just has to be *yours*.
3. **The Format:** Submit it via a simple Typeform/Google Form by May 28th.
4. **The Release:** On May 29th, the outputs will be collated into a high-contrast, black-and-white digital zine titled *BOTH HANDS FULL*. 

### Visual Guidelines for the Zine 
- **Typography:** Ransom note cutouts, typewriter fonts, Letraset rub-on letters.
- **Color Palette:** High contrast black and white (CMYK misaligment for digital versions). No smooth gradients.
- **Texture:** Heavy noise, film grain, scanned paper fibers, tape marks.

### Logistics
- **Submission Portal:** Setup a Lovable.dev or Typeform app.
- **Collation:** Kris and the CMVan team will run the submissions through a script that applies a unifying "halftone/xerox" image filter to all artwork.
- **Distribution:** Hosted as a PDF download for the entire CMVan email list, printed as a limited run of 50 staple-bound booklets for VIPs/speakers.
