# Punk Rock AI — slide image prompts (CMVan, May 1, 2026)

**Talk:** *Punk Rock AI: Xerox Machines, Stolen Work, and the Most Creative Year of My Life* — framed as a **creativity and life story** first. **AI is one chapter**, not the only lens on the wall.

**Aspect ratio:** **Every slide image is 16:9.** Append `--ar 16:9` to every generator call (including optional slides). No exceptions.

**Aesthetic:** DIY punk zine, black / white / **blood red**; borrow texture grammar from [`../../source-material/waiff-brazil/BOTH-HANDS-FULL-SLIDES.md`](../../source-material/waiff-brazil/BOTH-HANDS-FULL-SLIDES.md) — **look**, not BHF narrative.

**Deck map:** [`../../slides/deck-outline-with-notes.md`](../../slides/deck-outline-with-notes.md)

## Slide 1 — Title: PUNK ROCK AI (creativity riot, not “AI conference”)

**Prompt:**

> Wide 16:9 zine wall explosion: **contact sheets**, torn gig flyers, **35mm film leaders**, hand-labeled darkroom bottles, stapled community posters, one battered camera body, spray stencil — **no clean data-center hero shot**. Halftone, Xerox dirt, blood red accents only. Text MUST appear: largest "PUNK ROCK AI", subline "A CREATIVITY STORY — ONE LOUD CHAPTER IS TECH", smaller "XEROX · STOLEN WORK · MOST CREATIVE YEAR OF MY LIFE", stamp "CMVAN · MAY 1 2026". Feels like a life in rooms, not a product launch. --ar 16:9
