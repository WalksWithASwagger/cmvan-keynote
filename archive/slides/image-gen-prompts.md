## 1. Midjourney / DALL-E — hero trio (walk-in / title energy)

*Target aesthetic:* High-contrast B/W Xerox zine, **blood red** accents, **human craft** foreground (film, prints, flyers) — tech never the only subject.

**Prompt A — Life in the work (cassette + contact sheets):**

> Wide 16:9: battered 90s cassette "27 YEARS" in foreground, **blurred wall of pinned contact sheets and gig flyers** behind — evidence of a creative life, not a server farm. Thin chromatic fringe **only** on torn paper edges. Heavy halftone, red stamp "FINISH THE THING". --ar 16:9

**Prompt B — One chapter: prints meet the machine (not “AI wallpaper”):**

> Wide 16:9: **stack of loose 4x6 prints** sliding off a metal table toward a **single** cold server rack silhouette in deep background — **small** relative to the human prints. Red handprint on one print corner. Xerox grain, high contrast. Blank title safe area lower third. --ar 16:9

**Prompt C — Taste era (selector, no smartphone):**

> Wide 16:9: sound system stacks, selector mid-gesture, crowd shadow, **vinyl** catching red label circle. Margin chalk "TASTE IS THE JOB". Film grain, zine paste. --ar 16:9

## 3. HeyGen / ElevenLabs — dress rehearsal

*Goal:* Promo where **you** are the hook; AI arrives halfway, not line one.

**Voice (ElevenLabs):** cloned Kris; ~70% stability; optional PA grit.

**Avatar (HeyGen):** Casual Kris; dark or greenscreen; composite over **Prompt A** (life-in-the-work).

**Script:**

"I've spent my life in the messy part of making — darkrooms, deadlines, community rooms, the courage to ship something half-raw. Creativity, for me, has always been stubborn and physical.

Lately one chapter got loud: the machines. Built by giants, trained on us without asking — including about eighteen hundred of my photos. And somehow I'm also having the most creative year of my life. That's the tension I live in — not denial, not hype. Punk.

**May first**, Creative Mornings Vancouver — **Punk Rock AI**. Then we keep the bigger habit alive: **Release Day, May twenty-ninth** — ship the thing you hid. Generation is cheap. Taste is not. You coming?"
