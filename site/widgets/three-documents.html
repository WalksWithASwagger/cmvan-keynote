DOC 01

Personal AI policy

What will you use these tools for? What will you refuse?

  • What will I never use AI for?
  • What will I always use AI for?
  • What conditions does AI need to meet for me to use it?
  • Who do I owe attribution, money, or consent to?
  • What’s the smallest, most honest version of this policy?

DOC 02

Style and voice guide

What are the ten words you refuse to say? What does your work sound like when it’s really you? Ban the word delve.

  • Ten words I refuse to use.
  • Five references I love — books, films, voices, places.
  • “It never sounds like me when…” (3 examples).
  • Sentence rhythm I gravitate toward (short / long / fragmented).
  • What I want a reader to feel after the first paragraph.

DOC 03

Worldview document

Your values, your politics, your perspective. Are you a feminist? An anarchist? A punk? Five sentences is plenty.

  • What I believe about people.
  • What I believe about craft.
  • What I believe about justice and power.
  • What I will never outsource — even if I could.
  • One line that summarizes the rest.
