What just happened

Your browser captured raw audio with navigator.mediaDevices.getUserMedia and encoded it with MediaRecorder. That same blob, sent to ElevenLabs or Resemble or any of a dozen providers, would be enough to clone your voice well enough to fool your mother on a phone call.

What this booth doesn’t do

  • No upload. No fetch. No server.
  • No actual cloning. The download is just your raw recording.
  • No analytics on the audio. The page can’t hear you after the recording stops.

The point

Fifteen seconds is a voicemail greeting. Sixty seconds is a podcast intro. You leak more than that every week. The defense isn’t silence. It’s knowing the floor is this low and acting accordingly.
