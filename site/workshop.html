Three Documents.

One — a personal AI policy. Two — a style and voice guide. Three — a worldview. Five sentences each. Feed them to the model once and it stops handing you the average of the internet.

DOC 01

Personal AI policy

What will you use these tools for? What will you refuse?

  • What will I never use AI for?
  • What will I always use AI for?
  • Who do I owe attribution, money, or consent to?
DOC 02

Style and voice guide

Ten words you refuse. Five references you love. What sounds like you.

  • Ten words I refuse to use.
  • Five references I love — books, films, voices, places.
  • It never sounds like me when…
DOC 03

Worldview

Your values, your politics, your perspective. Five sentences is plenty.

  • What I believe about people.
  • What I believe about craft.
  • What I will never outsource — even if I could.

Bias Bingo.

Stop saying bias. Name what you’re seeing. Mark a cell when you’ve seen it ship in production. Complete a row or column and you have a checklist for your next stand-up.

Categories sourced from Algorithmic Justice League research and primary literature. Center cell = the room you’re in. Whoever’s missing is the bias you ship.
