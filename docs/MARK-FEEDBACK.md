## What Mark Is Actually Asking For

1. **AI is one chapter** — the talk should lead with Kris's 30-year creativity story. Photography, community building, open-source, teaching — these come first. AI shows up as the most recent and sharpest chapter.

2. **Both personas need to show** — the punk provocateur AND the thoughtful, empathetic, mature Kris. Mark sees that guy and says the room needs to. Not on stage, the "crack his whip" energy — but the nuance and empathy that's been developing since his 20s.

3. **Format is discussion-first** — Mark confirmed: "crowd has their time to chat first, then you engage." In practice: Kris poses a prompt, crowd discusses ~3–5 min, then Kris responds with truth bombs keyed to what the room actually raised.

4. **Hard work is the story** — AI is not a shortcut. Kris's practice is obsessive, iterative, meticulous. That's the punk condition. The work is the work.

5. **Timing: 20–25 min** — confirmed window.
