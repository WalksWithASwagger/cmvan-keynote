## Short Bio - Third Person (~150 words)

Techartist. Quasi-sage. Cyberpunk anti-hero from the future.

Kris Krüg builds bridges where human creativity and machine intelligence collide, creating weird, wonderful things neither could dream up alone.

As Executive Director of BC + AI Ecosystem Association, he has built one of Canada's most vibrant AI communities. 250+ founding members. 3,000+ people through the doors. A cross-disciplinary, genre-smashing, silo-busting gathering where academics and artists and builders find each other. Vancouver Magazine recognized this work in their 2025 Power 50.

As CTO of Indigenomics Institute, he weaves Indigenous wisdom with cutting-edge tech because ancestral patterns often hold keys to our digital future. Through The Upgrade AI, he has trained hundreds of creative professionals to collaborate with machines rather than be replaced by them.

He doesn't build systems that optimize humans. He crafts spaces where humans can be gloriously inefficient, creative, and alive.
