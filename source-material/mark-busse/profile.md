# Mark Busse – CreativeMornings Vancouver Co-Host, Likemind Organizer, Community Builder

## Summary

**Mark Busse** is a Vancouver community builder who co-hosts **CreativeMornings Vancouver** - now in its 15th year as Canada's first chapter of the global network spanning 255 cities in 70 countries with 2 million subscribers. Organizational leader, strategic advisor, storyteller, engagement specialist, certified leadership coach, & licensed officiant. At the Squatchie Awards (December 2025), Mark announced a historic milestone: on January 9, 2026, CreativeMornings Vancouver will become the first chapter globally to host an award-winning artist, filmmaker, actor, and director with Down syndrome, demonstrating the program's commitment to accessibility and inclusive creativity. Every month, CreativeMornings provides free breakfast, free music, and access to creative practitioners sharing their "relationship, their journey, their struggles with creativity" at the Vancouver Art Gallery.
