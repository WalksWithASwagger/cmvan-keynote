## KEY QUOTES (Exact Words)

**The Opening:**
"I find my relationship with AI completely non-consensual."

**The Both/And:**
"AI is trained on stolen work without consent. And I'm more creative, more productive, and more powerful than I've ever been in my whole life. Both statements are true."

**Name the Bias:**
"Stop saying bias. Name what you're seeing. Misogyny. Racism. Sexism. Not 'algorithmic challenges.'"

**Bias Laundering:**
"Discrimination that looks like math. 'The algorithm said so' carries weight that 'my gut feeling says so' doesn't."

**The Framing Bridge:**
"What's missing from the frame matters as much as what's in it. Training data works exactly the same way."

**The Governance:**
"If all the thoughtful, ethical, critical people opt out -- governance gets made by opportunists and true believers. That's worse."

**The Protester:**
"If you're not 20 years old, pissed off, and challenging things, you're not really paying attention."

**The Mirror:**
"AI is a mirror. It shows us who we've been -- our patterns, our discrimination, our historical inequities -- and amplifies them at planetary scale. The mirror doesn't lie. But it only shows what we put in front of it."

**The Soul:**
"Feed your soul, not the machine."

**The Closing:**
"I'm here. Both hands full. Walking forward. You coming?"
