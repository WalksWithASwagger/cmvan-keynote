# LaSalle College Vancouver — Speaker Opportunity Meeting

**Date:** October 3, 2025  
**Attendees:** Kris Krüg, Linda Folster (Manager of Career Services), Miriam  
**Subject:** Planning alumni event on AI's impact on creative careers

> **Note:** The original summary referenced "January 2024" as the proposed timing — this was a typo. The event being planned is **January 14, 2026**.

---

## Meeting Overview

- Meeting between Kris Krüg (AI expert/creative professional) and LaSalle College Vancouver representatives (Linda Folster, Manager of Career Services, and Miriam)
- Purpose: Discuss potential speaking engagement for Kris to present to LaSalle alumni about AI's impact on creative professions
- Proposed timing: January 2026 (avoiding Jan 28-30 when Kris is already booked)

### About LaSalle College Vancouver

- Formerly The Art Institute of Vancouver (acquired around 2017)
- Applied arts college offering programs in media arts and design fields
- Recently moved to a new campus near Renfrew Skytrain Station
- In early stages of launching alumni programming initiatives

### Speaking Engagement Proposal

- Topic: AI's impact on creative careers (high student interest noted during previous alumni panels)
- Target audience: LaSalle alumni (~100 attendees expected)
- Format: Presentation followed by networking opportunity
- Potential to open event to non-alumni community members for a fee
- Kris suggested this could launch a series of continuing education events

### Compensation Discussion

- LaSalle facing financial constraints due to immigration policy changes affecting international enrollment
- Proposed arrangement combining:
    - Modest honorarium
    - Access to LaSalle spaces for Kris's events
    - Photo/video documentation of the presentation
- Kris proposed potential sponsorship arrangement where LaSalle could sponsor his AI creativity events for 2026

### Broader Collaboration Possibilities

- Potential collaboration with AI labs at SFU or UBC
- Kris mentioned his organization and International Experiential Art Conference
- Kris informed LaSalle about Signals, a creative technology festival by DigiBC that might interest their media arts programs
