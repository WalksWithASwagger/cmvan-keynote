## Image Prompts

### 1. Both Hands Full — Hero Image
**For:** Article header, LinkedIn featured image
**Prompt:**
> A creative professional standing at the center of the frame, one hand raised on each side. The left hand holds abstract dark shapes representing concerns and fears (fragmented images, question marks, shadows). The right hand holds glowing abstract shapes representing possibility (light streams, connected nodes, creative tools). The figure stands confidently between both. Dark background with teal and purple accent lighting. Modern editorial illustration style, high contrast, conceptual portrait.

---

### 2. The Binary Trap — Rejected
**For:** Section on "Both are lazy" / the false binary
**Prompt:**
> Two opposing doors or pathways, one labeled with hype icons (rockets, sparkles, money signs) and one with doom icons (skull, broken tools, chains). Both doors are shown crossed out or fading. In the center, a third path opens forward with warm teal light. Dark background, high contrast, conceptual illustration showing rejection of binary thinking. Modern editorial style.

---

### 3. The Human Moat — Taste as Differentiator
**For:** Section on judgment, taste, discernment
**Prompt:**
> A creative professional surrounded by dozens of floating AI-generated outputs (images, documents, designs). They're selecting just one with a confident gesture while the others fade away. The selected piece glows with warm light. Visual metaphor for curation over generation. Dark background with teal and purple accents, modern conceptual illustration, editorial photography aesthetic.

---

### 4. Write for the Bot — Making Values Machine-Readable
**For:** Section on documenting worldview, style guides
**Prompt:**
> Hands typing on a keyboard with words and values floating up from the screen like particles or light streams, forming a constellation or knowledge graph above. The words include terms like "values," "style," "worldview," "judgment." The particles feed into a gentle AI presence represented as ambient light. Dark background, teal accent on the floating text, purple glow around the AI presence. Modern tech-forward illustration, warm human-AI collaboration theme.

---

### 5. Creative DNA — The Last Thing You'd Keep
**For:** Closing reflection section
**Prompt:**
> A close-up of a person's hands holding a small glowing core—their "creative DNA"—while around them, tools, tasks, and processes float away or dissolve. The glowing core pulses with warm light, representing the irreducible human essence. Meditative composition, centered focus, dark background with warm highlights on the hands and core. Conceptual portrait, editorial illustration style.
