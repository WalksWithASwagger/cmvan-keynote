# Future Proof Creatives — Relaunch Strategy

**Owner:** Kris Krüg
**Created:** 2026-04-10
**Target Launch:** Creative Mornings Vancouver, May 2026
**Status:** Planning

---

## The Opportunity

Three things are converging right now:

1. **Peter is fizzling out of upgrade.AI** — Kris is ready to carry the creative professional training forward independently
2. **Creative Mornings Vancouver** invited Kris to speak in May (theme: "Creativity") — a stage in front of Vancouver's creative community, with 15 years of credibility behind it
3. **Future Proof Creatives has unfinished business** — it was folded into upgrade.AI in September 2023, but the original mission — humanizing technology for creative professionals — is more relevant than ever

This is the moment to bring FPC back as Kris's personal training brand for creative professionals.

---

## What FPC Was (2023)

Future Proof Creatives was Kris's training and innovation initiative under MØTLEYKRÜG Media. It was:

- **Mission-driven:** "Humanize technology, infuse it with empathy, ethics, and emotion"
- **Community-first:** Monthly events, studio space, grassroots gatherings
- **Creatives-focused:** Designers, filmmakers, photographers, musicians, writers
- **Values-led:** "A tribe of experiment-hungry makers"
- **Academically validated:** BC Studies paper documented the community model

It was folded into upgrade.AI when Peter Bittner partnered with Kris through the Google News Initiative accelerator. The rationale at the time: "deeper fuel cells" — more resources, mentors, and a global network.

## What FPC Offers

### Flagship: Future Proof Creatives Certification (6 Weeks)

Carries forward the proven AI Upgrade for Creative Professionals curriculum:

- **Week 1:** Foundations — Mindset before mechanics, ethics from Day 1
- **Week 2:** Framework — POP Method (Persona, Objective, Parameters)
- **Week 3:** Reinforcement — Pattern recognition, meta-prompting
- **Week 4:** Construction — Building AI assistants, system prompts
- **Week 5:** Creation — Vibe coding, rapid prototyping (Lovable, Bolt)
- **Week 6:** Demonstration — Capstone presentations, deployment

**Format:** Live sessions + 2x 1:1 coaching + capstone project
**Cohort size:** 8-12 students
**Pricing:** ~$2,000-2,500 CAD (with government funding pathways at 50-100%)

### Supporting Offerings

- **1:1 Creative AI Coaching** — $400-500/session, packages available
- **Corporate Creative Workshops** — Half-day to multi-week for creative teams
- **Speaking & Keynotes** — "Creative Rebellion in an Age of Synthetic Everything"
- **Community Events** — Monthly gatherings (can partner with Creative Mornings, Vancouver AI Meetup)

## Creative Mornings Talk: The Launchpad

### Why This Stage Matters

- **Mark Busse** has been talking about this for a long time — deep trust and relationship
- **15 years of credibility** — Canada's first Creative Mornings chapter
- **Theme is "Creativity"** — perfect alignment with FPC's mission
- **The audience IS the market** — creative professionals in Vancouver
- **Precedent:** October 2025 BC+AI partnership event at Creative Mornings was a success
- **Global reach:** Creative Mornings spans 255 cities, 70 countries, 2M subscribers

### Talk Concept: "Future Proofing Your Creativity"

**Arc:** Personal journey from photographer → tech community builder → AI trainer → realizing that the creative professionals he's been working with aren't being replaced by AI — they're being amplified by it.

**Key beats:**

1. **The Fear** — "I've spent 20 years as a photographer. When AI image generation arrived, I thought my career was over."
2. **The Discovery** — What actually happened when creative professionals started using AI (real stories from 50+ alumni)
3. **The Framework** — How the best creatives are using AI: not as a replacement, but as a collaborator (POP Method, Worldview Guides)
4. **The Evidence** — Concrete outcomes: Paramount+ tools deployed globally, businesses launched in 6 weeks, a 17-year-old founding a community, Indigenous court cases analyzed
5. **The Invitation** — "I started Future Proof Creatives because I believe the most important AI applications won't come from tech companies — they'll come from creative professionals who understand the human experience. That's you."
6. **The Soft Launch** — Announce first FPC cohort, founding member pricing for Creative Mornings attendees

### Creative Mornings → FPC Pipeline

- Attendees get **founding member access** (echoing the October 2025 "Solidarity Over Discounts" model)
- Groups of 5+ from Creative Mornings join as a cohort-within-a-cohort
- Mark Busse as ongoing community partner (not just a one-time event)
