## Notes

- Written as a companion piece to the CMVan keynote "Both Hands Full" (Creative Mornings Vancouver, May 1, 2026)
- Spirit of the talk rather than literal recap: holding contradiction, the weirdos-first lineage, giving everything away, consent violation without collapse, swimming upstream as identity
- Picks up the "dead fish / live fish" Dr. Ronald Shaw quote (Sacramento youth pastor ~1992) as the closing image
- "Leaf blower" line echoes the mastery gym / taste / judgment section of the talk
- "The strange ones always catch the fire / before the money comes to town" — the Dada → Punk → DJs → Hip Hop → AI lineage compressed to two lines
